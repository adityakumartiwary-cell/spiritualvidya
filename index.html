The Bhagavad Gita

Divine Teachings on the Battlefield

As the great war was about to begin, Arjuna was overwhelmed with doubt and moral confusion. It was then that Lord Krishna delivered the timeless teachings known as the Bhagavad Gita.

"You have a right to perform your prescribed duty, but not to the fruits of action."
- Krishna to Arjuna
"Whatever happened was good. What is happening is good. What will happen shall also be good."
- Krishna's wisdom

Key Teachings:

  • The importance of performing one's duty without attachment to results
  • The eternal nature of the soul
  • Three paths to liberation: Knowledge, Devotion, and Action
  • The concept of dharma in difficult situations
